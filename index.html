The Wind and the Waves

Data collected from the
National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration's
National Data Buoy Center
(NDBC Website)

Requests updated data from NOAA's National Data Buoy Center for all active buoys. Buoy data is only updated every hour, so continuous refreshes are unnecessary. This will clear the local cache where data is stored and fills it with updated data. Last Updated date and time can be found in upper right corner of your screen.
